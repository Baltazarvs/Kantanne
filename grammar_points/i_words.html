以-Words

以-Words are special words in in Japanese that have multiple functions according to suffix attached. They are used for comparison, time, management, etc. They are usually attached directly to word they modify, which is mostly noun (number also as it is treated as noun).
There are 8 different 以-words:

  • 以上   (いじょう)
  • 以下   (いか)
  • 以前   (いぜん)
  • 以後   (いご)
  • 以外   (いがい)
  • 以内   (いない)
  • 以来   (いらい)
  • 以降   (いこう)

以上

以上 (いじょう) is 以-word that is used for specifying subset of things that are not less than specified from set. It translates as "not less than ~, ~ and more".
Mathematically, it can be represented as (≥).

例
１８歳以上の人は入れる。
People 18 or older can enter.
３年生以上の学生が宿題をする。
Students of 3rd grade and more are taking exam.

以下

以下 (いか) is 以-word that is used for specifying subset of things that are not greater than specified from set. It translates as "not greater than ~, ~ and less, no more than ...".
Mathematically, it can be represented as (≤).

例
１７歳以下の人はまだ子供だ。
People 17 and less are still kids.
その貨物は千キロ以下の重さを運べる
I can transport cargo not heavier than 1000 kg.

以前

以前 (いぜん) is 以-word that is used for time. It is basically same as 前 but more formal and is used in written text. It translates as "before, ago".

例
試験以前、コーヒーを飲みましょうか。
Let's drink coffee before the exam.
午後４時半以前は、留守にしないでください。
Don't leave house before 4:30 PM, please.

以後

以後 (いご) is 以-word that is used for time. It is basically same as 後 but more formal and is used in written text. It translates as "after, later".

例
試験以後、コーヒーを飲みましょうか。
Let's drink coffee before the exam.
午後４時半以後は、留守にしないでください。
Don't leave house before 4:30 PM, please.

以外

以外 (いがい) is 以-word that is used for excepting things from set. It can except multiple items from set. It translates as "except ..., excepting ..., but ...".

例
真理子さん以外は、会議室を出てください。
Everybody except Mariko-san leave the conference room, please.
一体なぜ誰も読み書きができないのか。田中さん以外。
Why in the world can nobody read and write except Mr.Tanaka?

以内

以内 (いない) is 以-word that is used for creating limit for time, amount, distance, etc. It translates as "within ..., in range of ...".

例
買い物で、3000円以内の物は買ってください。
When shopping, buy things that are within 3000 yen, please.
値段が3500円以内であれば、買ってくれ。
If price is within 3500 yen, buy it.

以来

以来 (いらい) is 以-word that is used for time where it specified range of time that is happening since certain event. It translates as "since ..., henceforth".

  • NOUN: ~ 以来
  • VERB: て-form + 以来

例
事故以来、運転しなくなった。
Since car accident, he stopped driving.
結婚して以来、毎日が楽しい
Since we got married, every day is fun.

以降

以降 (いこう) is 以-word is used for time and captures interval from certain event and afterwards. It translates as "on and after, from ... onward". Opposite of this is 以前.

  • NOUN: ~ 以降
  • VERB: て-form + 以降

例
アップデート以降バグが出た。
Bugs appeared after/since update.
中国に家を買って以降、父が悲しくなった。
Since/after I bought house in China, my father became sad.
